Changes a column position inside a dataset. It is possible to shift column either to the specified index or to the rightmost index. Other columns will be shifted appropriately.

Example: Given original dataset containing records about orders made by clients:

firstName lastName orderNumber totalItems totalCost
Alice Smith 1111 5 150
Bob Johnson 857 7 70
Alice Smith 1112 30 340
Alice Williams 505 1 170
Bob Johnson 858 3 370
Mary Williams 1543 1 15

It is possible to make orderNumber the last column:

firstName lastName totalItems totalCost orderNumber
Alice Smith 5 150 1111
Bob Johnson 7 70 857
Alice Smith 30 340 1112
Alice Williams 1 170 505
Bob Johnson 3 370 858
Mary Williams 1 15 1543

Parameters

Shift to index Shift to the rightmost position
